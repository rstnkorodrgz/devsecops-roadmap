> Track your progress, time spent, blockers, and wins every week.

## How to Use This Log

1. At the start of each week, copy the **Week Template** below and paste it at the top of this file
2. Fill in the `Phase` and `Week #` fields
3. Check off tasks as you complete them during the week
4. At the end of the week, fill in your reflection section
5. Update the progress table in `README.md`
